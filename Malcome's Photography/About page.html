ABOUT MALCOLM LISMORE

Hello, I'm Malcolm Lismore

This is how I introduce myself, let me officially introduce myself, My name is Malcolm Lismore and I am a freelance photographer from the beautiful North West coast of Scotland. I love to photograph the world independently at large and that is why I majored in nature.

For over ten years, I have been hiking and photographing around the Highlands of Scotland, from the cliffs to the birds of the Scottish coast. My work is devoted and inspired by my passion for nature.

Taking and making photos is how I interact with reality. From the mood of a foggy morning to the vibrant feast of wildlife, my goal is to create pictures with a story behind them.

I also specialize in weddings, portraits, and any event that requires professional services. For these events, I uphold the same fervor and thoroughness as with my everyday work, ensuring your special memories are beautifully captured.
